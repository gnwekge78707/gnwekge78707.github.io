About

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at CUHK MMLab, advised by Prof. Tianfan Xue. I got my B.E. degree in the School of Computer Science at Beihang University in 2024. During my undergraduate years, I also worked with Prof. Shanghang Zhang at Peking University.

I am currently studying and have previously worked on these topics:

  • Computational Photography: Image/Video Editing and Manipulation, 3D Reconstruction
  • Machine Vision Generalization: Domain Adaptation, Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning
